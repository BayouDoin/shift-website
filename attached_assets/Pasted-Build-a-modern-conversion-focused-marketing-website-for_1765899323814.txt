Build a modern, conversion-focused marketing website for an automation consultancy called Shift Systems.

⸻

Overall Intent

The site should feel slightly agency-styled but still founder-led, without centering on a personal brand. It should position Shift Systems as a serious systems partner trusted by large organizations and built for small and medium-sized businesses.

Primary goals:
	•	Convert warm traffic from referrals
	•	Support cold outbound credibility
	•	Act as a trust anchor for Upwork prospects
	•	Keep pricing consultative only

⸻

Tech Requirements
	•	Next.js (App Router) with TypeScript
	•	Tailwind CSS
	•	Mobile-first responsive design
	•	Fast load times and SEO optimized
	•	Clean, minimal, professional aesthetic
	•	Subtle hover and transition effects only

⸻

Brand & Positioning

Shift Systems builds enterprise-grade automation for small and medium-sized businesses.

Target audience:
	•	SMBs doing roughly $500k to $10M in revenue
	•	Small teams with manual sales and operational drag
	•	Founders and operators overwhelmed by follow-up and fragmented systems

Tone:
	•	Confident, strategic, calm
	•	Outcome-focused
	•	Avoid unnecessary technical jargon
